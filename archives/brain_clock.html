Publish Date:   2019-05-26
Read Count:  

平时我们所用的表，都是用来看时间的嘛；今天分享的这个表, 当然也是看时间，只不过需要计算才知道具体的时间。

其用意就是：锻炼大脑。同时，你可以设置它的出题难度。

让我想起之前一款闹钟app，只有答对题闹铃才会停止。当你解完题，那还有睡意啊。

Product Specifications

  • The Albert Math Clock
  • A clock that displays mathematical equations instead of those boring ol’ hours and minutes
  • A fun way to tell time for kids who are learning math and adults who like to keep their skills sharp
  • Simple timeless design (rimshot - it’s a clock, see?)
  • Changes calculations once per minute - or once every 10 seconds for the true math wizards
  • 4 levels of difficulty to challenge both kids and adults
  • Can switch between 12-hr and 24-hr formats
  • Can be used as a freestanding clock; also comes with a system for easy wall mounting and a ~10 ft. power cable
  • Works on both 110V and 220V (adapters for 5 different countries included)
  • Dimensions: 13.8” wide x 5.9” tall x 2” thick
