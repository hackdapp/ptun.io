To start off, thanks to the video sponsor: JLCPCB for providing pcbs to get the ball rolling on this project. JLCPCB Prototype for $2 (Any Color): https://jlcpcb.com

I’ve had these IV-21 vfd tubes for quite awhile and while they were originally geared towards small calculators I figured they’d make a great tiny desk clock. In this video we do a quick overview of the design and assembly and talk about a few issues I’ve run into in this iteration.
